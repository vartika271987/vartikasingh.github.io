About Me

I am a Senior Research Analyst at the International Food Policy Research Institute (IFPRI) with a PhD candidate at Humboldt University of Berlin. I specialize in sustainable food systems transformation, agricultural economics, and environmental policy.

My research focuses on the food-water-land nexus, exploring pathways to achieve sustainable development goals while ensuring food security and environmental sustainability in India and South Asia.
